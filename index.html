About Me

Hi I'm Curtis, I am a Graphic Designer. I started self-taught and now I'm currently majoring in Web Design and Development focusing on design at BYU-Idaho. I enjoy taking any idea and turning it into a reality. I love seeing the process from start to finish and witnessing the transformation that takes place, whether it be a project or a piece of art.

My goals are to be a part of a team-oriented organization where we can grow together and give the customer an honest and wonderful experience. I thrive on researching and learning different techniques that will stretch me beyond my limits. I enjoy artistic design and being creative.
