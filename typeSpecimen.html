Type Specimen Poster

Introduction: This poster is used to help promote the type Neue Haas Grotesk also known as Helvetica. I wanted Neue Haas Grotesk to be the most important element on the page, this is accomplished by making the size large and the colorÂ light blue. I wanted the flow of the poster to be vertical from top to bottom. The font styles on the left help the flow of the poster from top to bottom and the thin to thick font helps pull the viewer in. I left the rest of the font white to help stand out against the black background.

I started with a few rough draft designs and then made ten of those rough drafts into digital sketches. I wanted a design that could stand on its own and prove to catch the viewers eye before adding any color.
